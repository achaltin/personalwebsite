SOAR Leader | Illinois Institute of Technology

May 2017 to December 2017

After my second year, I interviewed through the Student Life Leader application process to become a Summer Orientation, Advising, and Registration (SOAR) Leader. During the summer, I received several days of training about the various campus resources available, and led groups at 8 overnight orientation sessions. Our mission statement was:

To create relationships and friendships by having intentional and goal-oriented interactions with new students and guests that not only serves as a catalyst for growth but also inspiration to achieve their goals.

I greatly enjoyed getting to be the first point of contact with students of the incoming class, and I feel the opportunity helped me gain a much better understanding of the university.
